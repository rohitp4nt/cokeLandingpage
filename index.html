Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fanta made of?

Fanta is made from carbonated water, high fructose corn syrup, citric acid, and natural flavors.

Is Fanta caffeine-free?

Yes, Fanta is naturally caffeine-free.

Where can I buy Fanta?

Fanta is available in supermarkets, convenience stores, and online retailers worldwide.
